How It Works.


Save for your next adventure automatically.

Connect your bank. Flyt then safely and securely identifies when it can safely put away money towards your goal. We never store your bank info so you can rest assured.

Flyt saves for you.

Flyt transfers money for your checking account 1- 5 times a week and put it towards your Flyt goal. Flyt automatically stops saving if your balance is below a certain amount.

Access your money at any time.

You can redirect funds from your goal back to your account at anytime. Just text Flyt and it will be returned within one business day.
